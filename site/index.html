Ascend AI Demo · Net-new

BoardVitals — Mastery Engine & Forgetfulness Forecast

Goes beyond a score — it watches quiz history at the concept level and knows what's solid, what's fading, and what was never really learned, then says what to do about it.

What it does

  • 1Live mastery state per concept — Strong, Fragile, or Mastered-but-at-risk — from accuracy, timing, repetition, and recovery.
  • 2Forgetfulness Forecast — surfaces "topics at risk of forgetting" before the student fails again.
  • 3One honest "next best action" after every session (review set / mixed practice / remediation / readiness check).
  • 4Explainable — "Last correct: 12 days ago · Topic difficulty: high · Reviewed once"; instructor Cohort View with one-click "Suggest intervention" on flagged students only.

Business objective

Lifts pass rates and daily engagement by telling each student what to review before they forget it — turning a passive question bank into a retention-and-readiness engine, and giving instructors early warning on at-risk learners.

AI Portfolio overlap

Consumes AI Content Ingestion (#16) and AI Tagging (#19/#21); the mastery engine itself is net-new, built over BoardVitals' own risk/recommendation ML.

Hatsize — Lab Authoring & Live Session AI

Two companions around the Hatsize labs platform: an AI that drafts a full lab from a plain-language description, and an in-session copilot that guides students without giving away answers.

AI Lab Blueprint Assistant

Authoring companion
  • 1Generates a title, 3–4 learning objectives, duration/difficulty tags, and the full VM topology.
  • 2Writes a 5-step flow with expected outcomes, plus a grading rubric (screenshot OCR, file diff, automated test, word count) with weights.
  • 3Preset prompt chips for quick starts (e.g. "CCNA VLAN lab", "Linux hardening").
  • 4Nothing publishes automatically — every draft is "Auto-generated · review before saving"; instructor Accepts, Edits, or Regenerates.

Business objective

Cuts lab authoring from hours to minutes, so instructors build more hands-on labs and the catalog grows without extra content-team headcount.

Open live demo →

Lab Copilot — Live Session Assistant

In-session companion
  • 1Tracks live context — which step ("Step 3 of 5"), last command, and a "possibly stuck" status.
  • 2Idle nudge: "Need a hint?" with three options — subtle hint, detailed explanation, or "Not now."
  • 3Socratic mode (on by default) — points at the real error and asks a guiding question back.
  • 4A sidebar, not a takeover — it observes and suggests, never touches lab state.

Business objective

Reduces mid-lab drop-off and support load by unblocking students in the moment — higher completion without more instructor time.

JBL — StudyBot & CRM Match

Two AI surfaces for Jones & Bartlett Learning: an in-book study partner grounded in the purchased title, and an account-matching engine for sales ops.

In-Book AI Study Partner ("StudyBot")

Direct overlap · #24
  • 1Source-grounded Q&A — quotes the exact passage, with a visible confidence score and expandable source excerpt.
  • 2Generates one-page summaries, 10-question quizzes, and flashcard sets, all traceable to book / chapter / page.
  • 3Study Collections — Claire suggests bundling a quiz, flashcards, and a summary into one saved collection.
  • 4Instructor review of generated items, plus admin governance — per-title AI toggle, usage logs, Legal-signed policy.

Business objective

Closes a competitive gap (VitalSource and Pearson already ship this) and deepens engagement across JBL's multi-portal catalog.

CRM ↔ Catalogue Account Match

Confirm first
  • 1Proposes mappings ("State University → jbl_acct_suid_987, confidence 0.92") from a CSV upload or a scheduled sync.
  • 2Flags conflicts (one lead matching multiple accounts) and suggests reconciliation rules (unify on email domain or campus code).
  • 3Evidence panel per match — email domain, billing address, recent order IDs, similarity score.
  • 4Nothing merges on its own — every proposal is accepted / edited / rejected; every action logged, auditable, and reversible.

Business objective

Removes the documented 10–15 min-per-request matching delay and improves sales/ops data quality and account analytics.

FISDAP — AI Shift Feedback Copilot

Reads a logged patient encounter and helps both sides at once — the student submits a complete log, and the instructor gets a drafted narrative to review and approve.

What it does

  • 1Real-time completeness check against the competency framework, before submission.
  • 2Guided fix flow — not just a red flag, but why the field matters and an example of a good entry.
  • 3Competency evidence map per domain — strong, partial, or not-captured.
  • 4Instructor shift queue sorted by urgency; the AI drafts and maps, the instructor edits and approves — the AI never grades or signs off.

Business objective

Saves instructor review time, raises documentation completeness, and speeds feedback turnaround — supporting accreditation and program outcomes.

AI Portfolio overlap

Mirrors the AI Tagging (#18–21) / Course Enablement (#23) pattern — classify against a taxonomy, confidence-scored, human review.

Swift River — Adaptive Clinical Simulation Engine

An AI layer over Swift River's 600+ scenarios that turns each fixed case into a personalized, unfolding journey.

What it does

  • 1Adapts in real time — strong learners get fewer hints and more complex deterioration; struggling learners get extra cues and slower escalation.
  • 2One base case serves beginner, intermediate, and advanced learners without duplicating content.
  • 3Faculty mode — class heatmap, common weak spots, AI-generated debrief questions, remediation groupings.
  • 4Admin mode — cohort trends, readiness-score distribution, at-risk flags; authoring rules where the AI proposes and the educator approves.

Business objective

Extends the value of the existing 600+ scenario library by serving every skill level from one case — better clinical-judgment outcomes with less content duplication.

AI Portfolio overlap

Analytics-and-recommendation surface like Course Enablement (#23) and AI Study Collections (#24); the product itself isn't in the portfolio.

Simulation Engineering — 3-D Web Simulation (Three.js)

A working browser 3-D simulation ("ATI Engage: Pharmacology — Dosage Calculations & Medication Errors, Lesson 10") that replaces the heavy Unity 3D build with a lighter web-native Three.js engine, wrapped in the Swift River shell.

What it does

  • 1The real Three.js Lesson 10 sim plays in-browser, with a persistent Adaptive Engine rail.
  • 2Concept mastery pills (Strong / Fragile / At-risk), a Forgetfulness Forecast with explainability, and a next-best-action button.
  • 3Debrief view — decision timeline, missed cues, judgment radar, AI reflection questions, and an AI-recommended remediation plan.
  • 4Restores the 3-D experience Unity gave up on, at web-native cost — no per-platform builds.

Business objective

Slashes 3-D simulation build and maintenance cost by replacing Unity's multi-platform pipeline with a web-native engine — faster time-to-market and no per-platform builds.

AI Portfolio overlap

Relates to Conversational AI Avatar (#22) — a web-native rendering layer for scripted clinical-conversation sims.
